About

The Breaking Academy.

Though breaking techniques students will start to build a "vocabulary" of skills and movements. Students will also develop strength, coordination, balance and gain gross motor skills in a fun and positive environment. Our classes welcome all ages and lessons will be determined by skill level.

Staff

World Class Educators.

The staff at The Breaking Academy LV is world class. Multiple world title holders who are also seasoned professional working dancers.

Etienne Carreira

Etienne Carreira has spent his breaking career traveling the world, battling, and teaching dance workshops.

He stared his professional dance career 24 years ago and has been breaking for 17 years. He is a 6x footwork champion and a founding member of the BattleBorn crew.

Throughout his career he has performed in many stage productions including: Toni Braxton's Revealed, The Beatles "LOVE" by Cirque Du Soleil, Splash, Jabbawockeez MUS.I.C., and is currently a dancer/B-boy in MJ ONE by Cirque Du Soleil.

❮ ❯

Kareem Gwinn

Known for his power and creativity, Kareem Gwinn has been dancing for 20 years and has won some of the biggest world competitions, including "Battle of the Year" and "Freestyle Session."

Kareem is a sponsored B-boy for Monster Energy and has traveled the world competing at the highest level. He has performed in the professional stage production The Beatles "LOVE" by Cirque Du Soleil, and looks forward to showing the world his continued growth and talent as a B-boy, professional dancer, and teacher.

❮ ❯

Guillaume Carreira

Hailing from Hawaii, Guillaume Carreira is a second-generation performer who started his professional dance career over 20 years ago.

Performing in countless stage productions and world tours, he has worked in all 50 states and 20 different countries with shows such as: Folies Bergere, STOMP (NYC), STOMP World Tour, Splash, Storm, Jabbawockeez MUS.I.C. and is currently a featured dancer in The Beatles "LOVE" by Cirque Du Soleil.
